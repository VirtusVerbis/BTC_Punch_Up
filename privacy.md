# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to **BTC Punch Up**.

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app displays publicly available Bitcoin-related data retrieved from third-party public APIs:

- **Binance** and **Coinbase** — real-time price and order-book volume (buy/sell) via WebSocket.
- **Mempool.space** — Bitcoin block height (tip), polled periodically.

No user-identifiable information is transmitted to these services.

The app does not require account registration, does not offer in-app purchases, and does not display advertisements.

No analytics, tracking technologies, or cookies are used.

This app is provided for informational and visual purposes only.
